# Fiction vs. Nonfiction

I've always preferred fictional books to non-fiction books. By non-fiction books, I'm talking specifically about books that attempt to teach humans more about themselves rather than history books or biographical books, like Grit by Angela Ducksworth. I think the reason I prefer fiction is that it requires active thinking to come to your conclusions, i.e. inspect why a character feels this way or performs this action. On the other hand, books like Grit kind of hand you the answer, and reading becomes more passive. That leads to less actual learning since
